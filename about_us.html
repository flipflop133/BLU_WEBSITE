About us

Introduction

Blu is not just an app, there’s a young and innovative team behind this project, with one goal, allowing both students and teachers to achieve their objectives. Students can find teachers available to give them courses near them and teachers can find students to give them quality courses. Blu is free, easy to use and respect your privacy.

Reasons for undertaking the project

All start because we were students and like most students, we often need help to understand our courses. But the catch was that it was always hard and tricky to find teachers who accept to give us courses. So here comes our idea to allow both teachers and students to meet together on an independent platform. Another problems were the price and the distance, so to solve both of them we decided to allow students to swipe teachers until they find the right one for them, close and at a fair price.

Objectives and constraints of the project

Our biggest problem was to ensure the quality of the courses given by the teachers. So to deal with it we decided to control the teachers certificates.

Identities of the main stakeholders

At the beginning the team was only composed of two members, François Bechet and Sofian Aboutaher. They were both sharing the same troubles to find help in their studies and they were both motivated to create their own project to solve this issue. Sofian Aboutaher is a Moroccan business man, he is the CEO of the BLU company. He comes from Casablanca in Morocco and now lives in Belgium. He’s an determined team leader, nothing can stop him and he managed to bring this whole team to where it is today. François Bechet is a Belgian computer scientist, he is the engineering manager. He comes from Arlon in Belgium. He is constantly learning to stay up to date with the latest technology stuff. He’s a bit sleep deprivatives but after all someone needs to stay awake to communicate with the rest of the world. Mamadou Lamine Bah is a Guinean civil engineer, he is the implementor of the team. He comes from Conakry in Guinea and now also lives in Belgium. He travels the world to bring new brilliant ideas to the team and share the team ideology. He’s also always trying to implement the app in newest country. He also ensure the right implementation of the ideas. Hippolyte Rentmeesters is a bright and reliable economist; he is the senior team builder. As the senior of the team, he avoids us the basic mistakes trough the creation process.
